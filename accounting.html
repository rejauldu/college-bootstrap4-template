Accounting and Payroll
COURSE DESCRIPTION

The Accounting and Payroll Administration program is designed to provide job-oriented training and skills to prepare students for the workplace environment. The program equips students with the fundamental principles and practices o a small business and corporate environment. Towards the end of the accounting component of the program, there is an in-house practicum where students will execute full-cycle accounting using a simulated company. In addition, students will finish all the courses required to be certified as Payroll Compliance Practitioner (PCP).

PCP Courses
  • Payroll Compliance Legislation
  • Payroll Fundamentals 1
  • Payroll Fundamentals 2

For more information on course requirements and practical work experience, please visit www.payroll.ca

COURSE
DURATION

The Accounting and Payroll Administration program consists of 54 full time weeks of classroom teaching and in-house practicum. Students will be able to choose between Morning and Evening classes.

COURSE
CREDENTIALS TYPE

Students will be given a diploma as an education credential for the completion of this course.
